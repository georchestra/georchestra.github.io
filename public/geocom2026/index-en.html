The PSC is pleased to announce that the 13th edition of geOcom, the annual meeting of geOrchestra users and developers, will take place from June 10 to 12, 2026 in Strasbourg at the invitation of DataGrandEst.

It will be preceded by a community sprint on June 8, 9, and 10.
