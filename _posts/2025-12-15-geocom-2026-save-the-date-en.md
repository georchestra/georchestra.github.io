The geOrchestra steering committee is pleased to announce that geOcom 2026 will be held in Strasbourg, from Wednesday, June 10th at 2:00 PM to Friday, June 12th at 12:00 PM.

It will be preceded by a community meeting, from Monday, June 8th at 2:00 PM to Wednesday, June 10th at 12:00 PM.

Further details, registration information, and the program will be available in early February, but we encourage you to save the dates now.
