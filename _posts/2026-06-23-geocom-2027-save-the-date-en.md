The geOrchestra steering committee is pleased to announce that geOcom 2027 will be held in Montpellier during the week of May 31 to June 4, 2027. Our host will be the French Agricultural Research Centre for International Development (CIRAD).


Further details regarding specifics, registration, and the program will follow in January or February, but please mark this week in your calendars for geOcom now.
