The PSC is pleased to announce that the **13th edition of geOcom**, the annual meeting of geOrchestra users and developers, will take place **from June 10th to 12th, 2026 in Strasbourg**, at the invitation of [DataGrandEst](https://www.datagrandest.fr).

It will be preceded by a **community sprint** on June 8th, 9th, and 10th.

The event is open to everyone!

<!--more-->

As usual, this will be an opportunity to discover new platforms, new players, current and future projects, and to build the roadmap for the coming year.

We therefore invite you to:

* Save these dates in your calendars
* Book your accommodation as soon as possible
* Tell us your expectations for the discussions
* Propose a presentation if you wish, via the mailing list or by writing to us (see contact at the bottom of the page)

<p>&nbsp;</p>
Find the registration form and the program on [the geOcom 2026 page](/en/geocom2026/).
